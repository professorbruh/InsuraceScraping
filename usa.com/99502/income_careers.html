As of 2010-2014, the per capita income of 99502 Zip Code is $37,916, which is higher than the state average of $33,129 and is higher than the national average of $28,555. 99502 Zip Code median household income is $87,973, which has grown by 41.74% since 2000. The median household income growth rate is higher than the state average rate of 39.28% and is much higher than the national average rate of 27.36%. On average 99502 Zip Code residents spend 19.7 minutes per day commuting to work, which is higher than the state average of 18.1 minutes and is lower than the national average of 25.7 minutes.
