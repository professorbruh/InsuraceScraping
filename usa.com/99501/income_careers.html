As of 2010-2014, the per capita income of 99501 Zip Code is $38,606, which is higher than the state average of $33,129 and is higher than the national average of $28,555. 99501 Zip Code median household income is $57,093, which has grown by 54.13% since 2000. The median household income growth rate is much higher than the state average rate of 39.28% and is much higher than the national average rate of 27.36%. On average 99501 Zip Code residents spend 16.0 minutes per day commuting to work, which is lower than the state average of 18.1 minutes and is lower than the national average of 25.7 minutes.
