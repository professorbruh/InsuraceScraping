As of 2010-2014, the total population of 99503 Zip Code is 14,661, which is 5.88% more than it was in 2000. The population growth rate is much lower than the state average rate of 16.17% and is much lower than the national average rate of 11.61%. The 99503 Zip Code population density is 708.57 people per square mile, which is much higher than the state average density of 1.09 people per square mile and is much higher than the national average density of 82.73 people per square mile. There is no dominant race in 99503 Zip Code.The average 99503 Zip Code education level is lower than the state average and is lower than the national average.
